About VIPER UNIGE

The VIPER group deals with the development of Machine Learning and Data Mining techniques. Our current research interests include:

  • Large-scale and High-dimensional issues in Machine Learning (e.g., Manifold Learning and Approximate Indexing)
  • Large-scale Data Mining (Big Data)
  • Large-scale Information Indexing (Distributed Indexing)

We collaborate closely with the Data Mining and Machine Learning (DMML) group of the University of Applied Sciences, Western Switzerland (HEG), and the MedGIFT group from the University of Applied Sciences, Sierre, Wallis (HEVs).
